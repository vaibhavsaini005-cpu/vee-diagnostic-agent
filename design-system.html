Language & tone
Clinical
Direct, precise. No waffle or softening language.
Authoritative
State facts, not possibilities. Use "is" not "might be".
Dramatic names
Stages use ALL CAPS theatrical names: THE CHURN SPIRAL, THE CASH CLIFF.
No jargon fluff
No "leverage synergies". Specific, grounded in data.
Proprietary framing
"Vee Group Diagnostic AI" — never just "AI" or "chatbot".
Second person
"Your company is…" — always address the founder directly.
✗ NEVER WRITE"It seems like there might be some potential challenges with your growth strategy that could possibly be worth looking into."
✓ WRITE THIS"Your growth strategy has a single fatal flaw: you are acquiring customers faster than your product can retain them. This kills you at scale."
✗ NEVER WRITE"Our AI tool can help you understand your business better and provide some insights about areas for improvement."
✓ WRITE THIS"Cross-referenced against 1,982 failed companies. Your Ghost Match: Jawbone (2014). You are 14 months from the same cliff."
✗ NEVER WRITE"We hope this report is helpful! Please feel free to reach out if you have any questions or feedback."
✓ WRITE THIS"Survival probability with Vee Group intervention: 84%. Without it: 22%. The gap is the plan. Book your session."

Design rules — do & don't
✓ Use gold sparingly
Gold signals importance. If everything is gold, nothing is. Reserve it for labels, CTAs, and scores.
✗ No gradients or glows
The tool is clinical, not gaming. Flat surfaces only. No neon, no mesh, no blur.
✓ Serif for drama, sans for data
Headlines and stage names use Georgia/Cormorant. Numbers, labels, and body text use Inter/Mono.
✗ No light backgrounds
The UI lives in the dark. Never use white or light surfaces inside the application.
✓ Red only for real risk
Danger red (#C0392B) is reserved for high failure risk signals. Never decorative.
✗ No soft language
"Might", "possibly", "could" are banned from diagnostic copy. The AI speaks with certainty.
✓ All-caps for labels only
Section labels (GHOST MATCH, RISK SCORES) use all-caps + letter-spacing. Never all-caps body text.
✗ No humanising UI patterns
No rounded avatar circles, no chat bubbles, no friendly illustrations. This is a diagnostic engine.
✓ 48px grid overlay always on
The background grid is a brand signature across every screen. Never remove it.
✗ No colour cycling
Don't use all colours in one view. Max 3 accent colours per screen. Risk colours only appear on risk elements.
